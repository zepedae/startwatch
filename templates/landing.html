An effort-based time-management tool
Checking off to-do list items is important, but for work that involves a lot of troubleshooting, creativity, or other forms of uncertainty, unforseen circumstances can make this difficult. Despite your considerable effort, these unchecked lists can create a sense of failure that can lead to burnout.
Startwatch solves this problem by using effort-based goals to help you manage your productivity across different projects. After setting working time goals for a project (e.g., 2 hours/day), users can create a "watch" to record their productivity each day. Productivity can then be visualized so users can evaluate their progress across time.
Achieving productivity goals using Startwatch leaves you with a sense of accomplishment even on the days when your to-do list doesn't change much. On days when your tasks are completed before your time goal is up, the strategy has the added bonus of encouraging you to work on those less time-sensitive, easy to avoid tasks.
