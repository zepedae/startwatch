Welcome to Startwatch

Startwatch is a time-management tools that addresses the shortcomings of to-do lists. Users can create a "watch" to record time spent working on a project. Working-time can then be visualized so users can evaluate their productivity across time.
Startwatch was born out of the feeling at the end of every day that I'd never done enough.
• Use in conjunction with a time goal and to do list every day
• On days you don't finish your to-do list you know you've made the effort
• On days you finish your to-do list before your time goal, spend a that extra time working on things you avoid adding to your to-do list for whatever reason
